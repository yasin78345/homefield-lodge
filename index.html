Find Us

Homefield Lodge is a 2-star hotel in West London, 1.7 km from Park Royal and 11 miles from London Heathrow Airport. The area is praised by guests for its convenient public transport, local restaurants, and easy access to central London attractions.

  • ✈️ London Heathrow Airport — 11 miles
